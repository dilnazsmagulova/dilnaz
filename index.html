Our values

Our baby-loving formulas are extensively tested with the support of pediatricians to ensure we address babies' and kids' specific needs and take extra care of delicate hair and sensitive skin
We performed eye irritation testing on our Gentle Shampoo, Gentle Conditioner and Bubble Bath for babies and kids of all ages to make the best of bath time without any tears.
We make skincare and makeup with love, for all. None of our products or formulas have or will ever be tested on animals.
We formulate without parabens, a controversial preservative widely used in the cosmetics industry, because your safety and health is our priority
